Falafel

Instructions

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onion, garlic, herbs (such as parsley and cilantro), spices (like cumin and coriander), flour, and salt.

  • Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until a coarse mixture forms.
  • Shape the mixture into small balls or patties.
  • Heat oil in a frying pan or deep fryer.
  • Fry the falafel until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with pita bread, salad, and tahini sauce.
  • Enjoy your homemade falafel!
