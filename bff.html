Sandwich

Instructions

Ingredients: Bread, protein (e.g., deli meat), condiments (e.g., mayonnaise, mustard), and any additional toppings you prefer (e.g., lettuce, tomato).

  • Take two slices of bread.
  • Spread condiments on one or both slices of bread.
  • Place the protein on one slice of bread
  • Add any additional toppings, such as lettuce and tomato.
  • Close the sandwich by placing the second slice of bread on top.
  • Press the sandwich gently together.
  • Enjoy your simple sandwich!
