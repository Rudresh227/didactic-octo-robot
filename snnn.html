French Fries

Instructions

Ingredients: Potatoes, oil (such as vegetable or canola oil), salt (optional), and any desired seasonings.

  • Preheat the oven to a high temperature, usually around 425°F (220°C).
  • Wash and peel the potatoes (optional), then cut them into uniform strips or wedges.
  • Place the cut potatoes in a bowl of cold water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  • Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.
  • Toss the potatoes with oil, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  • Spread the potatoes out in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are golden brown and crispy.
  • Season with salt or any desired seasonings while they are still hot.
