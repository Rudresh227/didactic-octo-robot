Pancake

Instructions

Ingredients: 1 cup pancake mix (readymade mix available in stores),3/4 cup milk,1 large egg,2 tablespoons vegetable oil or melted butter.

  • Gather pancake mix, milk or water, eggs, and desired toppings.
  • Mix pancake batter according to package instructions or from scratch.
  • Preheat non-stick skillet or griddle.
  • Pour batter onto skillet and cook until bubbles form
  • Then flip and cook the other side.
  • Repeat with remaining batter.
  • Serve hot with toppings.
  • Enjoy your pancakes!
