Fried Rice

Instructions

Ingredients: Cooked rice, vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers), protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu), garlic, eggs, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

  • Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  • Saut√© minced garlic for a minute.
  • Cook protein until done, then push to one side.
  • Scramble eggs on the empty side of the pan.
  • Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  • Mix in cooked rice and stir-fry to combine.
  • Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil, cook for a few more minutes, and serve hot.
