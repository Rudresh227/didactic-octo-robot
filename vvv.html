Chicken Nuggets

Instructions

Ingredients: Chicken breast, bread crumbs, flour, eggs, salt, and desired seasonings.

  • Cut chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  • Season chicken pieces with salt and desired seasonings.
  • Coat chicken pieces in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with bread crumbs.
  • Place coated chicken nuggets on a lined baking sheet.
  • Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through and crispy.
  • Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces.
  • Enjoy your homemade chicken nuggets!
