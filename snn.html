Popcorn

Instructions

Ingredients: Popcorn kernels, oil (such as vegetable or coconut oil), and salt (optional)

  • Place a large pot with a tight-fitting lid on the stove over medium heat.
  • Add the oil to the pot and let it heat up.
  • Once the oil is hot, add the popcorn kernels to the pot and cover with the lid.
  • Shake the pot gently to ensure even heat distribution.
  • Listen for the popping sound. Once the popping slows down to a few seconds between pops, remove the pot from the heat.
  • Let the popcorn sit for a minute to allow any remaining kernels to pop.
  • Carefully remove the lid, season with salt if desired, and transfer the popcorn to a bowl.
