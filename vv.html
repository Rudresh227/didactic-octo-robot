Fish Fingers

Instructions

Ingredients: Fish fillets, bread crumbs, flour, eggs, salt, and desired seasonings

  • Cut fish fillets into strips, season with salt and seasonings.
  • Coat fish strips in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with bread crumbs.
  • Place coated fish strips on lined baking sheet.
  • Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  • Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
  • Enjoy your homemade fish fingers!
